Who Exactly Am I?

I am simply someone who wants to advocate for privacy for everyone. This whole passion of mine started from one project in high school, and the rest is history. I'll admit that it is a bit of a weird for someone like me to major in Statistics and Machine Learning. I see that stopping these invasive practices as impossible, so what can be done to preserve privacy under them? My entire desire to learn about Statistics and Machine Learning was to see if privacy could coexist. I want to protect privacy for everyone, because that's how highly I value my own.
